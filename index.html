About Tiny Crafts 🧶

Our Passion

Welcome to Tiny Crafts! We specialize in creating the cutest, highest-quality needle-felted keyrings. Whether you love woodland animals, pop-culture icons, or sweet treats, we have a tiny companion for your keys or backpack.

Why Choose Us?

  • Handmade Charm: Every single item is uniquely crafted, meaning no two pandas or sloths are exactly alike!
  • Soft & Durable: Our felted wool is tightly packed to endure the daily bumping of keys and bags.
  • Independent Makers: By shopping with us, you are supporting independent artists.
